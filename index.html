Horizontal Splitting | H 24 E

Automatic horizontal splitting of bonded foam or semi-rigid PU foams

706,000-726,000 €

ORDER NOW
A "sister version" of the H 31, the H 24 E bandknife splitting machine cuts blocks and slabs into layers economically and fully automatically. The machine is constructed from cast iron, making it extremely solid and long-lasting. It produces consistent results with very low tolerances and always delivers a high cutting quality when processing bonded foam, polyester, semi-rigid PU foams and similar materials.



Secure materials for minimal tolerances


A corundum-coated aluminum table reliably secures the material to be split. The material can be split very precisely into layers with tolerances (up to 10 mm [0.4 in]) of +/- 0.1 mm [0.004 in]. The following applies for models in the H series: The bandknife is automatically adjusted with the aid of a light barrier. This means that a consistent cutting quality is achieved over the entire lifetime of the bandknife.



Achieving precise cuts over the entire service life


The H 24 E splitting machine is available in various working widths, working lengths and cutting heights according to your requirements and applications. The specially formed bandknife beam ensures the highest level of precision when processing your materials. Furthermore, the automatic bandknife adjustment means that the bandknife remains in the ideal position as it wears, thereby guaranteeing consistently precise cutting results.
